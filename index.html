The Selector

Defining the Sound of Underground House

MJ is one of the UK's most respected female underground selectors — a DJ for over 20 years, predominantly on the UK Underground Music scene, pushing the sounds of House, UK & US Garage, Breakbeat and Funk.

Based in Birmingham, bookings take MJ across the UK and internationally. Best known for her energy, passion, and ability to keep a dancefloor alive from first track to last, she's been recognised as one of the most influential female DJs in the sounds she represents.

Festivals and events across the UK, Netherlands, Europe and Africa such as Amsterdams ADE Dance Weekender and more.

I play what I feel and I feel what I play — how can you expect a room full of people to lose themselves in the music if you as the DJ don't even feel what you're playing?

Radio & Shows

Back2House Radio — initially internet-based and now broadcasting to over ¾ of the globe — has gained support from some of the most respected names in House Music history.

Throughout her career MJ has held shows on D3EP Radio, House FM London, and guest mixed for stations spanning Canada, Switzerland, Italy, Chicago and Amsterdam.
